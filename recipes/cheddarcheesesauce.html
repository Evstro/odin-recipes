Cheddar Cheese Sauce

This keto friendly cheese sauce is made of just a few simple ingredients: cheddar cheese, heavy cream, almond milk (or any low carb friendly milk of choice), and butter. Thatâ€™s it!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Cheddar Cheese (shredded)
  • 1/4 cup Heavy Cream
  • 2 tbsp Unsweetened Almond Milk (or any milk of choice, or more cream)
  • 1 tbsp Butter (optional, but recommended for a richer sauce)

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a saucepan over low heat. Heat, stirring frequently, until the cheese mixture is smooth. Be careful not to overheat or the cheese will burn.
